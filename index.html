Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: the immersive world of video games.

By merging video games and exercise, he found a way to improve his health and happiness. GamerXise was born out of blood, sweat, tears, and a lot of trial and error, and is now being packaged to share with and benefit the whole world.

Our Services

GamerXise Monthly

A monthly subscription providing new workouts every month. Get guided workouts and resources to make it easy to do while gaming, as well as educational resources to teach you the basics, and bonuses to improve results.

GamerXise Client

For those with specific goals or needing a tailored program, GamerXise Client offers a personal trainer to assess, guide, create, and monitor your program, adjusting it as needed. Ideal for game skill goals, health challenges, and specific physical targets.

GamerXise Programming

Get a semi-personalized program designed by a personal trainer, with monthly updates and adjustments. This service includes an initial assessment and program fee, followed by a lower monthly rate for unlimited updates.
