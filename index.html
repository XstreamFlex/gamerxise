Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder who, as an ADHD and TBI survivor, struggled with chronic migraines and maintaining healthy exercise habits. He realized that the immersive nature of video games could be leveraged to create a unique fitness solution. Through trial and error, GamerXise was born?a program that merges video games and exercise to improve both physical and mental health. Our mission is to share this innovative approach with the world, helping gamers achieve their health and fitness goals while enjoying their favorite pastime.
